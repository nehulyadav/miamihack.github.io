What is MiamiHack?

We are first timers, but you are not. We're excited to have you! We provide out of state travel reimbursement. Get friends, apply early and we buy your plane ticket! A student run hackathon to take part in a weekend fun of coding, networking and engaging with problems that shape our lives. It is open to all students to learn and grow, especially computer science majors. We have tracks that you can choose from to make it the best experience for you all!


Miami Hack 2016 is the first ever hackathon that allows you to pursue ideas that you think will change the world.

FAQ

Will I get travel reimbursement?

Yes, we provide out of state travel reimbursement. Get friends, apply early and we buy your plane ticket!

Why do I come to your first hackathon?

This hackathon is special because we have amazing sponsors, our friends coming from MIT, Harvard, Stanford, and we have worked really hard to make it a learning experience for you
